# Final Research Summary

## Objective
Research leading B2B SaaS practitioners to identify emerging trends in B2B content strategy, demand generation, AI search, and audience building.

## Experts Analyzed
- Chris Walker
- Ross Simmonds
- Dave Gerhardt
- Amanda Natividad
- Samu Kovacs
- Adam Robinson
- April Dunford
- Kevin Indig
- Tom Hunt
- Lenny Rachitsky

## Key Findings

### Educational content outperforms promotional content.
### Founder-led content builds trust faster.
### Distribution matters more than content creation.
### Long-form content compounds over time.
### AI search is changing discovery.
### Audience ownership is becoming a strategic advantage.

## Conclusion
Successful B2B SaaS companies increasingly rely on educational content ecosystems, audience ownership, AI discoverability, and long-term content compounding strategies.
