# Research Notes

## Initial Observations

YouTube content strategy for B2B SaaS is not only about gaining views. The stronger pattern across experts is that video supports trust-building, education, content repurposing, category awareness, and demand creation.

## Early Themes

- B2B YouTube should focus on buyer education, not viral entertainment.
- Founder-led and expert-led content works well because trust matters in B2B.
- Long-form video can be repurposed into LinkedIn posts, newsletters, short clips, and sales enablement content.
- The best videos answer real buyer questions.
- Distribution matters as much as production quality.

## Questions to Explore

- What type of YouTube content works best for B2B SaaS?
- How should SaaS companies measure YouTube success?
- How can long-form video support pipeline?
- How can AI tools speed up research, scripting, and repurposing?
