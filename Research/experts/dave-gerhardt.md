Title:
The Best B2B Influencer Marketing Strategy in 2026

Date:
2026

Link:

Key Insights:

-It's brand, not direct response: Don't expect a single influencer post to instantly drive sales; it's a long-term trust and signaling play.

-Buy the content, not the audience: Use creators as a cost-effective content production engine to feed your own ads and email campaigns.

-Story beats big budgets: You don't need A-list celebrities; look for micro-influencers whose personal backstory naturally fits your product.

-Loosen creative control: Giving creators freedom to design the concepts keeps the content authentic and significantly lowers their fee.

-Human trust beats AI noise: As AI changes traditional search, B2B buyers are moving toward real human faces, video content, and platforms like     Reddit.

Link:
https://www.linkedin.com/posts/davegerhardt_we-polled-540-b2b-marketers-on-how-they-feel-activity-7470420490621464576-MWC_

Key Insights:

-Hype vs. reality gap: Most real-world B2B marketers are adopting AI gradually and practically, despite the aggressive "total revolution" hype on social media.

-Repetitive tasks win: Marketers use AI primarily to speed up administrative workflows and formatting, rather than replacing deep creative strategy.

-Shift to custom tools: Teams are moving away from generic prompts and building hyper-focused custom tools trained on their own customer data.

-Testing without mastery: Most marketing teams are actively experimenting with different platforms, but very few have completely transformed their core strategies.

-Human voice dominates: Because basic text is now a free commodity, authentic personal brands and real customer insights are the only ways to stand out
