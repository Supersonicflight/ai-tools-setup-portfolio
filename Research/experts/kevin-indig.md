Key Insights:

- Traffic decoupled from revenue: Raw organic traffic is no longer the definitive metric for business success as AI answer engines directly satisfy informational intent.

- Leverage self-sustaining growth loops: Rather than relying on linear content production, build scalable programmatic systems that compounding-wise reinvest returns into the channel.

- Prioritize business revenue attribution: Marketers must implement incrementality testing and leading indicators to map search impact directly to revenue pipeline.
